*** ADMIN PANEL ***

1) The login button on the TopBar will consist of a dropdown of 2 
options, i.e. Client & Admin. Admin credentials will be set from the
backend itself, no need to register an admin via the GUI/website.

2) Admin Panel will show all the leads/requests that have been 
created by the clients including all the info they have submitted in
the forms.

3) Consultant/Manager can validate the cliet's details and documents
 via this panel and make decision on whether to accept or reject a
particular lead.

4) The Client will be notified via 3 ways.
	1. Client Dashboard
	2. Notifications (Button in TopBar)
	3. E-mail
